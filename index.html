Who I Am

           
           
               
                                   
               
                   

                        Hi, I'm Ankit Kailey — a Computer Science student at Rowan University with                          an unwavering passion for Data Science, Machine Learning, and Backend Development. My journey                          in tech began with curiosity about how data can tell stories and drive decisions, and has since                          expanded into building real-world backend systems and APIs.                    

                   

                        Throughout my academic journey, I've immersed myself in the world of data — from statistical                          analysis and predictive modeling to building interactive dashboards that translate complex                          datasets into actionable insights. Recently, I've expanded into backend development, building                          REST APIs with FastAPI, managing databases with SQLAlchemy,                          and applying version control with Git & GitHub.                    

                   

                        Every project I undertake is driven by a simple question:                          How can technology solve real-world problems? Whether it's analyzing 47,000+                          job postings, clustering agricultural commodities, or building a fully functional student                          management API — I approach each challenge with analytical rigor and creative thinking.                    

                   

                        Looking ahead to Summer 2026 and beyond, I'm eager to contribute to                          organizations that value innovation, analytical thinking, and continuous learning. My goal                          isn't just to be a developer or data scientist — it's to be someone who makes a difference                          through technology.
